I really couldn't come up with a name for this project. so, in the spirit of a man from Texas who might also fall into the same state of confusion that I did, I decided to call it "DooHickey". DooHickey is a tool that lets you doo-hickey!!!. Okay, that's obviously a joke. BUt, DooHickey is actually a tool that uses custom-trained OCR models to extract text from images and documents and let's you use the extracted data to train ML models with zero code.
